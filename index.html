Privacy Policy for Save Image As...

Effective Date: 24/11/2024

Introduction

Welcome to Save Image As.... Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting any personal information you may share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data collected by the Save Image As... Chrome extension and ensures transparency about our practices.

By using this extension, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

What Information Does the Extension Collect?

Save Image As... does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or identifiable information about its users. The extension operates entirely within your browser, and all processing (such as image conversion) is performed locally on your device.

Permissions Requested and Their Purpose

  • contextMenus: To add the "Save Image As..." option to the right-click context menu for images.
  • downloads: To save the converted images to your device.
  • activeTab: To inject scripts into the active tab for processing images.
  • scripting: To execute scripts for image processing (e.g., conversion to different formats).
  • host_permissions: To access image URLs for conversion from any website.

These permissions are solely used to enable the functionality of the extension and enhance your experience. No data is collected or shared with third parties.

How Does the Extension Handle Data?

All actions performed by the extension, including image conversion and saving, occur entirely on your device. No data is sent to external servers. The extension does not track or monitor your browsing activity, and no user data is collected or shared.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with or use any third-party services that may collect data. All functionality is built into the extension and performed locally in your browser.
